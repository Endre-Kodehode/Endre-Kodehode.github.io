WHO ARE WE?

YUMMY SUSHI is the place to go if you are looking for classic style Japanese Sushi restaurant in the city of New York. Located at 210 E 44th St, the simple but elegant place has been serving delectable sushi and authentic Japanese food that would satisfy even the most sophisticated diner.

FOOD

Authentic and cooked to traditional recipes, our food is gimmick free and focuses on exceptional flavours and beautiful presentation. Our fresh handmade Japanese sushi is created from fish delivered daily to our kitchens. The sushi menu includes some of the most popular varieties, but please ask if you would like something special.

DRINKS

Japanese food is best enjoyed with a complementing beverage. Whether it's an ice cold beer, flask of sake or a super healthy green tea, our drinks are the perfect partners to classic Japanese dishes.
